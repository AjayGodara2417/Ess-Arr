FAQs

Do Ess Arr air coolers come with a warranty?

Yes, Our air coolers come equipped with a manufacturer's warranty to ensure customer satisfaction and total peace of mind. The warranty period may vary depending on the specific model and can be found in the product documentation or on the website. Make sure to register your product and retain the purchase invoice for warranty claims.

Can I use an air cooler in a closed room?

Ess Arr air coolers are advised to be used in places with proper cross-ventilation or when placed near an open window or door. They work best in such circumstances as coolers require a constant flow of fresh air to expel humid hair and maintain cooling efficiency. In the case of using our air coolers in a closed room, the humidity levels are expected to rise, therefore reducing the cooling effect.

How often should I clean the air cooler?

Once you buy our air cooler online, regular cleaning is essential to maintain optimal performance and prevent the buildup of dust, minerals, and bacteria. For longevity purposes, it is recommended to clean the cooling pads, water tank, and filters every two weeks or as specified.

Can I use air coolers outdoors?

Air coolers are designed primarily for indoor use. However, certain portable air coolers with durable construction and weather-resistant features can be used in covered outdoor areas like patios or balconies. Ensure the cooler is protected from direct exposure to rain or harsh weather conditions.

Can I use air coolers in areas with high humidity?

Air coolers work best in dry or arid climates, where they can effectively reduce the temperature and increase comfort. In areas with high humidity, the cooling effect may be limited as the air already holds significant moisture. However, some air coolers with adjustable humidity controls or dedicated dehumidifier functions can still provide relief in such conditions.

Are Ess Arr air coolers energy-efficient?

Yes, our air coolers are designed to be energy-efficient and save on electricity bills. Air coolers have a significant advantage in terms of energy efficiency when compared to conventional air conditioning systems. This is possible because do not have compressor, the primary power-consuming element in traditional cooling systems, enabling air coolers to cool the room with minimal energy consumption.
